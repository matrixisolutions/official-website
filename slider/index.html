ASTHRA 2017

Asthra is a two day intercollegiate technical fest of St.Joseph's College of Engineering and Technology, Palai. Asthra - 2017 is to be held on 22nd and 23rd of February 2017. There are a variety of events ranging from technical events to smaller fun events. The aim of Asthra is to creat a platform for students from various colleges to interact and to assess themselves by participating in various competitions.
